How to Apply for a Birth Certifcate

Procedure

  • Apply Online

    • Click on the right menu and start a new application

  • Visit Vital Statistic Unit
    1. Go to the Vital Statistics Unit to obtain the certificates.
    2. Fill up the request form.
    3. Pay the fee
